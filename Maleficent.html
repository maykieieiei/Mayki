Maleficent Mistress Evil's Introduction

Once upon a time... or perhaps you remember this story about a Fey named Maleficent.

She had powers like none other in this world with big, black and powerful wings that can bring her up higher than the clouds,
so powerful and strong it can crush a man's skull within seconds.

Her beautiful green eyes, pale skin and long black horns curved outwards with a pointy end was her signiture look.
A staff that contained a power beyond this world held in her right hand,
with her cape draped over her shoulders to hiding the scars from the betrayal of her first love.
A pet raven, or rather a sidekick named Diaval that can turn itself into a human
with Maleficent's command and a flick of her hand, always around her or resting on her shoulder.
The silhouette of Maleficent was feared by men who dared to step foot into her kingdom.
A flicker of her hands can destroy much in seconds. She was hunted by men and knights outside her kingdom, The Moors.

Maleficent Mistress of Evil

Once upon a time, there was two kingdoms that were the worst of neighbors.
Where one kingdom lived a greedy king who wanted to rule over them, and the other kingdom called Moors,
where different strange creatures lived and was ruled and protected by a powerful fairy called Maleficent.
Maleficent was relaxing under a tree, curing broken branches using her fairy powers to cure nature when one of the Spriggans,
also known as tree warriors, came to her in an alerting manner that they have encountered a young thief who stole a jewel in her kingdom.
She quickly fled to the scene and met Stefan, where their love story started. Stefan visiting Maleficent as they explore the moors together.
Soon, Maleficent fell in love for the first time at her young age, sixteen.

They grew up together in secret, Maleficent being hunted by Stefan's kind, the humans.
The king's last wish who fell sick after the war of attempting to take over the moors was to collect Maleficent's head, declaring that
whoever would serve him the head of the fairy would be the new heir of the kingdom. Stefan hearing all of this and wished to have that power came
with a plan. Knowing he could fool Maleficent into submission, he met up with her and bonded with her like the old times and gave her a drink mixed
with something else. Maleficent then fell unconscious in Stefan's arms. Stefan's plan was to serve the king Maleficent's head but the love he felt
for her was more than his desire to have power. He decided to cut her precious, big and powerful wings and left her unconscious.
He presented Maleficent's wings to the king and soon, he was crowned as the new king and had a daughter named Aurora. Maleficent was livid.

Maleficent arrived to Aurora's intrduction to the public unannouced. Taking her revenge, anger and the
deep betrayal she felt onto Stefan's daughter, Aurora. Cursing her, "Before the sun sets on her sixteenth birthday, she will prick her finger
on the spindle of the spinning wheel and fall into a sleep like death, a sleep from which she will never awaken." Stefan fell to his knees and begged
for Maleficent's forgiveness. "Alright, She can be woken from her death sleep. But only by... a true love's kiss." She growled and laughed then left
the new born cursed for the rest of her life. The new born grew up with beautiful blonde hair, blue eyes, pale skin and a very loving voice with a very caring nature.

Aurora, the cursed princess lived away from the castle with three fairies that took care of her for 15 years,
Aurora then stumbled on the borders of moors, seeing Maleficent's silhouette with nothing else in her eyes but fascination, Aurora's gentleness softened
Malificent and soon, they bonded. Aurora then found out that she was cursed, asking Maleficent if she knew anything about her curse that she didn't know Maleficent
was the one who cursed her. When Aurora found out Maleficent cursed her, she distanced herself as she felt hurt and betrayed by a fairy that she considered
her fairy-god-mother that looked after her for most of her living years. Maleficent regreted giving Aurora the curse, despite her anger towards Aurora's father,
King Stefan, she knew Aurora never deserved the curse. The sunset that was supposed to be Aurora's night of curse, Maleficent went to her, attempting to save her
from the curse she placed in Aurora.

She arrived at Aurora's castle too late, she was already laying in bed, sleeping like death with no true love to
to break the curse. Maleficent's love for the child was over powering her anger towards King Stefan. I will not ask your forgiveness because what I have done to you
is unforgivable... I was so lost in hatred and revenge. Sweet Aurora, you stole what was left of my heart. And now I have lost you forever."

Maleficent then kissed Aurora's forehead, full of love and sympathy that broke her curse.
Aurora awoke and forgave Maleficent and soon, Aurora helped Maleficent get her wings back that King Stefan had cut and kept in the
castle's basement. Maleficent gained her precious wings back and declared Aurora to be the first Human to rule over her kingdom, the Moors.

Then they lived happily ever after.

The End.

The Script to Maleficent Mistress of Evil is here!
A beautiful, pure-hearted young woman, Maleficent has an idyllic life growing up in a peaceable forest kingdom, until one day when an invading army threatens the harmony
of the land. Maleficent rises to be the land's fiercest protector, but she ultimately suffers a ruthless betrayalan act that begins to turn her pure heart to stone. Bent on revenge, Maleficent faces a battle with the invading king's successor and, as a result, places a curse upon his newborn infant Aurora. As the child grows, Maleficent realizes that
Aurora holds the key to peace in the kingdom - and perhaps to Maleficent's true happiness as well.
